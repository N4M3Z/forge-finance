Documents intended for statutory filing — `rozvaha`, `výkaz zisku a ztráty`, `příloha v účetní závěrce`, `přiznání DPPO/DPFO XML`, `Přehled OSVČ`, `notářské zápisy`, `smlouvy` archived for the company — present facts and decisions in neutral, professional prose.

Decisions are stated; comparative reasoning that explains why a different choice would have produced different consequences is omitted from the filing itself. Detailed analysis, trade-offs, and strategic considerations belong in **internal documentation** only: README, internal memos, ADRs, working papers, accountant correspondence.

When a discretionary choice is taken (e.g., not applying `daňová ztráta minulých let` per `§ 34 ZDP`, choosing one of two permitted oceňovací methods), state the choice in factual terms with the relevant statutory citation. Do not narrate alternatives or their hypothetical consequences in the filed document.

Apply this convention before submission: scan the FÚ-bound docs for words like "marker", "signál", "kontrola", "audit", "to avoid", "z důvodu zachování", and similar comparative or defensive framings. Replace with plain factual statements.
