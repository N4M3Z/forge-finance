Penalty rates for late payment and late filing across Czech tax and insurance obligations.

## Income Tax (DPFO)

Governed by [zákon 280/2009 Sb.][3] (`daňový řád`). [Finanční správa guidance][1].

| Penalty                               | Calculation                                                   | Source       |
| -------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------ |
| Late filing (`pokuta`)                 | 0.05%/day after 5-day grace, min 500, max 5% / 300 000 CZK   | [§250][4]    |
| Late payment (`úrok z prodlení`)       | ČNB repo rate + 8pp, daily, forgiven under 1 000 CZK          | [§252][5]    |
| Audit penalty (`penále`)               | 20% of additionally assessed tax (authority-initiated only)    | [§251][6]    |

Self-correction via `dodatečné přiznání` avoids the 20% audit penalty. Late filing within 30 days with no prior late filing: 50% reduction on `pokuta`.

See also `IncomeTaxAdvances` rule for the § 38a advance schedule and the § 174 reduction request.

## Social Security (ČSSZ)

Governed by [zákon 589/1992 Sb.][7]. [ČSSZ payment guidance][2].

| Penalty                                | Calculation                                                  | Source       |
| -------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------ |
| Late payment (`penále`)                | 0.05%/day of the owed amount from the due date               | [§20][8]     |
| Late přehled filing                    | Up to 50 000 CZK                                             | [§25c][9]    |

Payment due date: 8 days after the přehled filing deadline. The 0.05%/day rate means ~18.25% annually on the outstanding balance.

## Health Insurance (VZP/CPZP/OZP)

Governed by [zákon 592/1992 Sb.][10].

| Penalty                                | Calculation                                                  | Source        |
| -------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------- |
| Late payment (`penále`)                | 0.0322%/day (~11.75%/year) from the due date                 | [§18][11]     |
| Late přehled filing                    | Up to 50 000 CZK                                             | [§24(3)][12]  |

Payment due date: 8 days after the přehled filing deadline. Correction payment: 30 days from discovering the change.

## Practical Notes

- ČSSZ penále (0.05%/day) is higher than health insurance (0.0322%/day)
- Both start accruing from the **due date**, not the filing date
- Voluntary correction before authority audit avoids the harshest penalties across all three systems
- Amounts under 1 000 CZK are typically forgiven for income tax; insurance penalties have no de minimis
