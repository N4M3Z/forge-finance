Czech-specific catalogue of tax-return entries whose treatment is interpretive enough to invite `Finanční úřad` (FÚ) follow-up questions. Apply alongside the universal AuditSignalDisclosure rule — that rule defines the disclosure protocol; this rule lists what to disclose for Czech filings.

## DPPO markers (corporate tax)

| Marker                                                                                                              | Compliance consideration                                                                                                                                                                                                                              | Audit-safe alternative                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| `ř.40` `Náklady neuznatelné` — large amount on a single line                                                        | Invites the question "what is this and why is it `nedaňový`?". Common for receivable writeoffs, fines, gifts.                                                                                                                                       | Avoid the underlying writeoff entirely (leave receivable on books) or restructure as `postoupení pohledávky` per `§24(2)(s)`.                                                                                                                       |
| `ř.230` `Odečet daňové ztráty minulých let`                                                                         | Per [§38r odst. 2 ZDP][zdp38r], the `prekluzivní lhůta` for the loss-origin period is **automatically extended** to the last year the loss was usable plus 3 years. Applying an old loss puts the loss-origin year back in scope for review.        | Skip the deduction (`ř.230 = 0`) and pay higher tax. Combine with [§38r odst. 4 ZDP][zdp38r] `oznámení o vzdání se práva uplatnit daňovou ztrátu` filed in the loss-origin year to fully shut the extension.                                       |
| `Daň > VH před zdaněním` (tax owed exceeds book profit)                                                             | Implies large `nedaňový` adjustments on `ř.40`. Reviewers will trace the writeoff.                                                                                                                                                                  | Restructure so book profit ≥ tax owed: don't book the `nedaňový` writeoff (leave the underlying balance on books), or reclassify if law allows.                                                                                                     |
| Multi-year loss streak interrupted by a single profitable year with `ř.230` deduction wiping the new profit         | Pattern reads as engineered.                                                                                                                                                                                                                        | Do not apply old losses against the first profitable year — pay tax and let the older `daňová ztráta` expire (e.g. 2020 loss expires after 2025).                                                                                                   |
| Accounting-unit category mismatch — `s.r.o.` claiming `mikro účetní jednotka` while reporting revenue or assets above limits | Triggers re-categorization and back-fill demands.                                                                                                                                                                                                   | Verify category against `§1b` ZoÚ thresholds before each filing. Two consecutive years above thresholds promotes to next category.                                                                                                                  |
| Late `dodatečné přiznání` reducing prior-year tax                                                                   | Reads as opportunistic correction.                                                                                                                                                                                                                  | If a real correction is needed, file as soon as possible after discovery and document the discovery event.                                                                                                                                          |

## DPFO markers (personal tax)

| Marker                                                                                                      | Compliance consideration                                                                         | Audit-safe alternative                                                                                          |
| ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| `Příloha 3` foreign tax credit on `§6` employment income with `metoda zápočtu` when treaty allows `vynětí`  | Treaty plus `§38f` ZDP override sequencing matters; common error.                                | Verify treaty plus `§38f` override; document the chosen method. Wrong method choice triggers correction request.  |
| `§7` `paušální výdaje` at maximum cap with no cross-check against actual receipts                           | If receipts contradict the cap claim, recovery plus penalty.                                     | Use real expenses where they exceed `paušál`; document both.                                                      |
| `§15a` aggregate cap exceeded across multiple retirement products                                           | Combined `LTIP` plus pension plus life insurance > 48 000 CZK is a frequent pre-2024 trap.       | Keep running tally of `§15a` aggregate before claiming.                                                           |

## Cross-filing markers

| Marker                                                                                                | Compliance consideration                                                          | Audit-safe alternative                                                                                                       |
| ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Mismatch between `DPFO` `§7` revenue and `Přehled OSVČ` filed with `ČSSZ` or health insurer           | All three filings derive from the same income; mismatch triggers cross-check.     | Use the FilingWorkflow rule sequencing — file `DPFO` first, then derive insurance `přehledy` from the locked tax base.         |
| Claiming `§15` mortgage interest while not registered as owner of the property                        | Verifiable against `katastr nemovitostí`.                                         | Only claim when property ownership matches the filer.                                                                          |

## Default disclosure protocol

When recommending any item from the tables above, follow the AuditSignalDisclosure rule's two-column comparison format. Default to the audit-safe alternative for small Czech `s.r.o.` filings (annual revenue under 1 mil CZK) and routine personal returns.
